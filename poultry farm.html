Our Story

Fresh Fields Poultry has been raising chickens with care and respect. We believe in sustainable farming practices that prioritize animal welfare and produce the finest quality poultry products.

Our chickens roam free on open pastures, fed a natural diet without hormones or antibiotics. The result? Tender, flavorful chicken and rich, golden yolked eggs that families trust.
